About Me

Hi! I'm Muhammad Al-amin and I enjoy creating user interfaces that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2021 when I watched some tutorial on HTML & CSS in youtube. Since then I never stop learning and exploring new things.

I have graduated in Management Studies, BBA at University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. But, I found myself an extremely passionate about web development so I enrolled in six-months long Professional Web Development course at Programming Hero instructed by Jhankar Mahbub who is an expert in web development, where I learned the fundamental of Web Development. Besides this, I continuously explore documentation, articles, tutorials, books to increase my expertise.
